Short Bio

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Prof. Jiawei Hanâ€™s Data Mining Group (DMG) at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Previously, I obtained my Master degree at Fudan University in China. Currently, my research focuses on natural language understanding to build knowledge-aware intelligent systems and facilitate human-computer interaction with data analysis. In particular, I am excited about the following topics:

  • Large Language Model Alignment: align large language models with user-generated instructions to enhance the responsiveness of AI systems.

  • Executable Language Grounding: ground language instructions into code or actions executable in real-world environments.

  • On-Demand Information Seeking: create models capable of on-the-fly adaptation to seek structured information as demanded by users.
